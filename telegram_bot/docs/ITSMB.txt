# ⚙️ 4. Scheduling Logic (CORE PART)

You cannot skip structure here.

## Step 1: Expand Courses

Convert:

> “Course with 3 credit hours”

Into:

> 3 sessions per week × sections

---

## Step 2: Separate Courses

* Lab courses
* Non-lab courses

---

## Step 3: Assign LABS FIRST ⚠️

Why? Labs are limited.

### Rules:

* Only assign to lab rooms
* Avoid conflicts:

  * same room
  * same section

### Priority idea:

```
4th year → 3rd → 2nd
```

---

## Step 4: Assign Classrooms

Same idea:

* find free room
* find free slot
* check no conflict

---

## Step 5: Conflict Rules

Always check:

* A section cannot have 2 classes at same time
* A room cannot have 2 classes at same time

---

## Step 6: Fairness (IMPORTANT)

Track per section:

* morning slots
* afternoon slots

Balance them:

```
if section A has too many mornings → give next slot to B
```

* Greedy algorithm:
